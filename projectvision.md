## THE ONE SENTENCE VISION

> *To eliminate the guesswork from dining by giving every restaurant customer a beautiful, visual window into the food they are about to order — and giving every restaurant owner a tool that pays for itself every single month.*

---

## THE PROBLEM WE EXIST TO SOLVE

Walk into any restaurant in the world and you will face the same invisible problem.

The menu is a list of words. Maybe a short description. Sometimes a price. But no picture. No colour. No sense of portion size, presentation, or what the dish actually looks and feels like on a plate.

So customers do what humans naturally do when faced with uncertainty — they play it safe. They order the same thing they ordered last time. They avoid the unfamiliar. They skip the special. They choose the chicken because they know what chicken looks like and they are not sure about the sea bass.

The restaurant loses. The customer loses. And nobody realises it is happening because it has always been this way.

**We are here to change that.**

---

## THE INSIGHT BEHIND THE PRODUCT

People eat with their eyes first.

Every food marketer, every chef, every restaurant photographer knows this. When you can see food — really see it, in high quality, beautifully lit, true to life — you want it. The decision to order becomes easy, confident, and exciting rather than hesitant and safe.

The evidence is not theoretical. Restaurants that use visual menus consistently see:
- Higher average spend per table
- More adventurous ordering across the full menu
- Faster decision making — tables turn quicker
- Customers who feel more satisfied because what arrived matched what they expected

The problem has never been that restaurants do not want this. The problem is that no one has made it simple, affordable, and beautiful enough to deploy at scale.

**That is what we are building.**

---

## WHAT WE ARE BUILDING

A two-part digital product delivered as a complete turnkey package:

### The Customer Experience
A locked, branded tablet device that sits on the table or is brought by a waiter on request. Customers pick it up and browse high quality photographs of every dish, drink and dessert the restaurant offers. No app to download. No QR code to scan. No phone required. Just pick it up and see the food.

Customers who prefer the element of surprise can choose **Surprise Me** mode — names and descriptions only, no photographs. The choice belongs to the customer, not the technology.

### The Restaurant Control Panel
A simple web-based admin dashboard that any restaurant manager can learn in under five minutes. Add a dish, upload a photo, set a price, hit save. The device updates automatically the next time it connects to WiFi. No technical support needed. No phone calls to us. Full control, always.

### The Device
A premium Android tablet, locked to this application only. Configured by our team before delivery. Branded to the restaurant's identity — their logo, their colours, their welcome message. Ruggedised for the restaurant environment. Works completely offline once content is loaded.

## OUR POSITIONING

### What We Are Not
We are not a QR menu. QR menus ask the customer to do work — find their phone, open the camera, scan a code, navigate a browser, hope the WiFi works, manage their battery. We remove every one of those steps.

We are not an ordering system. We are not a payment platform. We are not trying to replace the waiter or the paper menu. We do one thing exceptionally well: we show customers what the food looks like before they order it.

### What Makes Us Different

Restaurants pay a monthly fee. We retain ownership of the hardware. If a device is damaged we swap it. Predictable recurring income from day one.

## PRINCIPLES — HOW WE BUILD AND SELL

These are not rules. They are beliefs that guide every decision we make.

**1. Simple beats clever**
Every feature we add must earn its place. If it makes the product harder to use or harder to explain, it does not belong in this version. We build the simplest thing that solves the problem completely.

**2. The demo is the pitch**
No slide deck closes a deal. Handing a restaurant owner a beautiful working device and watching their face when they see it — that closes deals. Every product decision we make serves the demo moment.

**3. Recurring over one-time**
A business that starts from zero every month is fragile. Every decision we make should move us toward more predictable, recurring revenue. The lease model is not just a pricing choice — it is a survival strategy.

**4. Solve the photography problem**
Every restaurant that wants this product but does not have food photography will stall and never come back. We remove that obstacle before it becomes one. Photography is part of onboarding. Always.

**5. Local before global**
We will dominate Coventry and the West Midlands before we think about anywhere else. Deep roots in one market generate case studies, referrals, and reputation that no marketing budget can buy.

**6. The restaurant wins first**
If the restaurant does not see a clear return — more orders, higher spend, faster tables — the product has failed regardless of how beautiful it looks. We sell outcomes, not technology.

**7. Never lose a deal on price**
Two tiers exist for a reason. Essential and Premium cover the full range of what restaurants are willing to spend. When someone says it is too expensive, we have an answer. We do not walk away.

# Product Vision & Strategic Goals
## L'Étoile Dorée Luxury Menu Kiosk System

---

## 1. Executive Summary & Vision

**L'Étoile Dorée** is an exquisite, high-performance visual menu and branding management system designed exclusively for premium, tableside hospitality devices. In high-end culinary establishments, guests do not merely eat; they experience. However, traditional paper text listings and cold, friction-heavy QR-code phone navigations disrupt this curated sensory flow. 

**Our Vision:** To transform the tableside guest journey into a highly engaging visual gallery. By presenting large, gorgeous, pixel-perfect photograph-focused culinary catalogs on fine, specialized physical tablets, we engage the customer's eyes first—eliminating ordering friction, celebrating culinary mastery, and driving average table spend up by 15-20%.

---

## 2. Strategic Pillars & Goals

### 视觉级体验 • High-Fidelity Visual Engagement
*   **The Power of Sight:** Replace archaic plain-text lists with a responsive, media-rich visual grid that displays high-fidelity gastrophotography, delicate texture close-ups, and meticulously detailed presentation layers.
*   **Aesthetic Discipline:** Maintain a gorgeous high-contrast dark palette (`#0D0D0D` and warm `#C9A84C` gold tones) crafted with sophisticated typography pairings, generous negative space, and elegant micro-animations.

### 两端联动 • Tablet-First & Owner-Empowered Architecture
*   **True Tablet Optimization:** Provide a luxurious Customer Tablet View designed specifically for landscape tablet viewports, featuring optimized touch targets, sidecar category bars, and layout-preserving detail modals.
*   **Owner Admin Command Console:** Empower sommeliers, pastry chefs, and general managers with a responsive configuration dashboard to add new recipes instantly, customize active availability, toggle regional allergen markers, and immediately synchronize the active fleet of tablet devices.

### 离线优先 • Elite Real-Time Durability
*   **Fault-Tolerant Client State:** Ensure the Customer Kiosk functions smoothly offline. The application caches active menu configurations, allergy data lists, and UI state securely, avoiding connection dropouts or fragile Wi-Fi failures during service.
*   **Seamless Diagnostics:** Maintain live system heartbeat verification and clear diagnostic modules to ensure staff are always aware of connectivity and configuration health.

---

## 3. Product Principles

1.  **De-cluttered Immersiveness:** Maintain zero "tech-larping" or pseudo-intellectual system chatter in the customer-facing interface. No telemetry streams, raw container logs, or network metrics cluttering the guest's curated culinary frame.
2.  **Graceful Recovery & Safety:** Automatic inactivity timers return the device to the primary welcome branding looping splash screen when guests leave, preparing the tablet instantly for the next table party.
3.  **UK & Global Allergen Compliance:** Prioritize customer trust with visually integrated allergen-declaration badges (Celery, Gluten, Crustaceans, Eggs, Fish, Lupin, Milk, Molluscs, Mustard, Nuts, Peanuts, Sesame, Soya, Sulphites) built directly into the administrative and client-modals architectures.
